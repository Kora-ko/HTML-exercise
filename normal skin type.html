What is Normal Skin?

Normal skin is well-balanced — not too oily, not too dry. It has a smooth texture, small pores, an even tone, and rarely experiences breakouts or irritation. People with normal skin typically enjoy low-maintenance care, but still benefit from consistent protection and hydration to keep their skin healthy and glowing.

Why Do You Have Normal Skin?

  • Genetics: You may have naturally balanced oil and water levels.

  • Healthy lifestyle & diet: Contributes to overall skin health.

  • Mild climate: Not too hot or cold helps maintain skin balance.

  • Minimal exposure to irritants: Such as harsh products or pollution.

BEST INGREDIENTS FOR NORMAL SKIN

Hyaluronic Acid

: Maintains hydration without heaviness.

Vitamin C

: Brightens skin and prevents dullness.

Niacinamide

: Supports skin barrier and smooths texture.

Peptides

: Boost collagen for long-term health.

Green Tea Extract

: Antioxidant and anti-aging benefits.

Pantheon (Vitamin B5)

: Calms and softens skin.

Recommended Skincare Routine

Day
  1. Gentle Cleanser – Keeps your skin fresh and clean.

  2. Antioxidant Serum (like Vitamin C) – Protects from daily damage.

  3. Lightweight Moisturizer – Hydrates and locks in nutrients.

  4. Broad-Spectrum Sunscreen SPF 30+ – Protects against UV damage.
Night
  1. Cleanser – Removes dirt and makeup.

  2. Hydrating Toner or Essence – Maintains moisture.

  3. Nourishing Serum (Niacinamide or Peptides) – For long-term skin strength.

  4. Night Cream or Gel Moisturizer – Keeps skin balanced overnight.
